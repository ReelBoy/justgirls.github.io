Just Girls

Just Girls are the perfect embodiment of the title "Indie Rock". The quartet formed when guitarist Jayson HaslamBrock saw Bassist, Kelsey Melvin, and Drummer, Kamilah Palmer, at an open mic night and asked them to jam on some songs he'd been working on. After adding Vocalist Amie Falkner to the group, the band's chemistry was undeniable.

The band infuses 90's indie music with infectious melodies to create music both distorted and catchy.

Just Girls' indie mentality bleeds into every aspect of the band's activities. From writing songs on homemade guitars, to hosting concerts at local schools and RV parks, as well as self producing and engineering all of their music.
